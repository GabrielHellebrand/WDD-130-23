Here's some reviews from some of the people who have explored the wonders of Rexburg's River Rafting.

River Rafting is very fun, when I was on the water it felt like I was back home in Florida except for less gators to ruin your trip and bless your thanksgiving. I wish Lenny could've been there, he did professional gator tossing until he lost his arms in a motorcycle accident, thanks Obama. Anyways, I gotta get off the computer because another gator got in the house and I need to find the shoval, thanks for listening. - Bahama Stan The Florida Man

Water is of the Devil - Jedidiah Stone

I'm a dad with a daughter of datin' age. Every time my daughter brings home a bo', I take him out to this here river. On the water, I speculate how deep the river is and wonder if anyone searches for the bodies at the bottom. It's a little father bonding with his daughter's boyfriend time, he gets the message, that was until my daughter got pregnant, 0 out of 5, don't recommend. - Steve Gunderson

I don't river raft anymore after I learned that the water makes the frogs gay. - Ron Jones

My family had a great river rafting trip, the staff was friendly, polite and my wife and twelve kids had fun, although we needed a bigger boat. - Nephi McConkie
